Volunteer!

Star of the North is made possible by the generous donations of time and money from fellow members of the community. If you are interested in volunteering for Star of the North, we can use volunteers for a variety of tasks, big and small, including but not limited to:

  • Decorations
  • Marketing
  • Sponsorship and grant-writing
  • Day-of volunteer work: on-deck captains, registration, set-up and tear-down

In addition, we are always looking for sponsors for this wonderful event. If you are interested in volunteering or making a tax-deductible donation to Star of the North, please contact us at fest@upartnerdance.org for more information.
