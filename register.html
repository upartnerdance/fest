Pricing

The registration form will calculate a price for you, but here's the gist:

  • All prices are are per person unless otherwise noted.
  • The Competitor Package includes unlimited amateur and MP event entries, admission to all sessions, and a program.
  • A student is anyone who is under the age of 18 or has a valid college ID.

Limits

When registering, the form will disable some options and tell you why. Here's how it will limit you:

  • Each person may enter up to 2 consecutive age levels.
  • Each person may enter up to 2 consecutive proficiency levels per style.
  • You may not dance mixed proficiency and amateur with the same partner.
