Pricing

  • All prices are per person unless otherwise noted.
  • All competitors receive unlimited amateur and Mixed Proficiency event entries, admission to all sessions, and a program.
  • If you are dancing only Mixed Proficiency and only as the more proficient partner, your event entries are free and you may spectate at all sessions for free.
  • If you are only dancing in one event and that event is Mixed Proficiency, your event entry and ticket to that session are free.
  • A student is anyone who is under the age of 18, has a valid college ID, or is an alumnus currently affiliated with a student group
  • Lodging with University students and/or financial assistance is available for schools that would experience financial hardship. Financial assistance is awarded on a case-by-case basis. For more information, please contact us at info@upartnerdance.org.

Limits

  • Each person may enter each event only once.
  • Each person may enter up to 2 consecutive proficiency levels per style.
  • You may not dance mixed proficiency and amateur with the same partner.
  • A Note for college teams: we will make exceptions to these limitations on a case-by-case basis in order for all members of your team to dance as much as is possible. Please contact our registration at info@upartnerdance.org for any assistance.
  • Costumes are allowed in Silver and Above. No Costumes will be allowed in Newcomer, Beginning Bronze or Advanced Bronze. This will be strictly enforced.

Dance Fest is

a low-cost, beginner-friendly dancesport competition where:

  • Dancers and Spectators alike can enjoy an atmosphere of friendliness, comraderie, and fun unlike any competition in the Midwest
  • New Competitors can see all that dancesport has to offer in a friendly, welcoming atmosphere without breaking the bank.
  • Experienced competitors can compete against some of the nation's best dancers in all styles and levels
  • All competitors and spectators can sharpen their skills with free seminars held throughout the event
