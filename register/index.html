Pricing

  • All prices are are per person unless otherwise noted.
  • All competitors receive unlimited amateur and MP event entries, admission to all sessions, and a program.
  • If you are dancing only MP and only as the more proficient partner, your event entries are free and you may spectate at all sessions for free.
  • If you are only dancing in one event and that event is MP, your event entry and ticket to that session are free.
  • A student is anyone who is under the age of 18 or has a valid college ID.

 

Adult Student
Competitors 80 40
Admission to one session 10
Program 5 each

Limits

  • Each person may enter each event only once.
  • Each person may enter up to 2 consecutive proficiency levels per style.
  • You may not dance mixed proficiency and amateur with the same partner.
